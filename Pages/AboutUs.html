Making sustainability simple

In our busy, modern lives it may seem that we have too much to worry about ourselves to really focus on moving towards more sustainable living. However, we built this site to highlight that it can actually be very easy to make small changes at home that can have a big impact. Many of us now live in compact, urban spaces and we have focused on several key elements of the modern, urban living space to demonstrate how we can make these spaces more harmonious with the environment and more eco friendly.

Many eco-friendly sites focus on muted, earthy tones but we have chosen to go with a vibrant, sunny site to highlight that sustainable living can be engaging, bright and doesn't have to only be done in one way. Across each of the living spaces featured we discuss sustainable swaps, useful products and interesting ways that we can incorporate a more eco-friendly outlook into the everyday. We hope you enjoy browsing our site as much as we enjoyed making it.
